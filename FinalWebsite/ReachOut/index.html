REACH OUT


An application to better connect individuals from the Western world with the wants and needs of the Syrian refugees, in an attempt to restore the humanity of the displaced population.

ABOUT THE APP

Reach Out will focus on fostering collaboration between refugees, their host countries, and those who wish to aid them. Refugees will communicate their needs in an anonymous setting. In response, interested parties can react to these needs through outreach and donations. Reach Out has both a local and global scope as it involves neighbors of the refugees and people from other countries wanting to help. The application will include forums for refugees and helpers to share their ideas.
